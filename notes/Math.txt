Math is an object with arithmetic functions. Here are some examples:
    > Math.abs(-2)
    2

    > Math.pow(3, 2)  // 3 to the power of 2
    9

    > Math.max(2, -1, 5)
    5

    > Math.round(1.9)
    2

    > Math.PI  // pre-defined constant for π
    3.141592653589793

    > Math.cos(Math.PI)  // compute the cosine for 180°
    -1
